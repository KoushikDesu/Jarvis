☁️ Cloud Training (Google Colab)

Since training complex models requires massive GPU horsepower, we use Google Colab's Free GPU Cloud (Tesla T4) to train Jarvis from scratch. Weights are automatically saved to your Google Drive.

Setup Instructions:

  • Download the notebook file below.
  • Upload it to Google Colab.
  • Set your runtime to T4 GPU (Runtime > Change runtime type).
  • Run all cells (it mounts Google Drive automatically).
  • Right-click the generated checkpoint.pt on your Google Drive, click Share, set to "Anyone with link can view", and paste the URL in the Settings tab to sync weights to this website!
